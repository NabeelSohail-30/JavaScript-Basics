DOM (Document Object Module)

When a web page is loaded, the browser creates a Document Object Model of the page.

DOM Methods and Properties

  • document.all
  • document.documentElement (Returns All Elements)
  • document.head (Returns Head Objects)
  • document.title (Get/Set Doc Title)
  • document.body (Returns HTML Body Objects)
  • document.images (Returns all Images Object in Array)
  • document.links (Returns all Links Object in Array)
  • document.forms (Returns all Form Object in Array)
  • document.doctype (Returns doctype)
  • document.URL (Returns Document URL)
  • document.baseURI (Returns Document URL)
  • document.domain (Returns Host Name/Domain Name)
  • document.getElementById("Id Name") (Returns Single HTML Element)
  • document.getElementByClassName("Class Name") (Returns Array of HTML Element)
  • document.getElementByTagName("Tag Name") (Returns Array of HTML Element)

Our Aim & Objectives

We work hard to ensure that everyone in our school family has the best possible chance to do as well as they can. We want every child to make the most of his opportunities for learning and to develop good social skills, as key stepping stones for his future.
We aim to make every child's time at our school a happy and an enjoyable experience that will lay good foundations not only for higher studies, but will also give them the skills, confidence and feeling of self worth that will stay with them throughout his life, to enable them to become a good citizen and a caring adult.
